FAQ

Can I try TIDAL for free?

If you haven't tried TIDAL before, you'll be able to sign up for a 30 day free trial. TIDAL's free gives you complete, ad-free access to TIDAL's lossless catalog and streaming features at no cost.

Can I bring my playlists on other services to TIDAL?

Yes, we want your transition to TIDAL to be as smooth as possible. Start importing your favourite playlists here.

What plan types does TIDAL offer?

TIDAL's most popular plan is the Individual plan. In addition to that, we offer a high-value Family plan (for you plus up to 5 members), and a discounted Student plan.
